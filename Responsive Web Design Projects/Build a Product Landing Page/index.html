TFT Coaching

Here at Sasso Stark Industries, we have a rooster of 10 pro players delivering personalized high quality coaching to help you improve your gameplay. Each of them has a guaranteed minimum Diamond placement and many of them are Challengers. We guarantee quality and results and we offer 24/7 live support.






Choose your pack


Base

The Base Pack includes a one-hour call during which your own game will be analyzed and, in the remaining time, you can discuss how to improve.
You will have the possibility to choose any day for your call, weekends excluded. A player from our rooster will accept your order as soon as possible.

Intermediate

In addition to the benefits of the Base Pack, you have a second call lasting one hour where our professional player will comment on your game in real time.
You can choose at will the day and time in which to carry out each call, without limits. An available player from our rooster will fulfill your request no later than two days.

Pro

Get all the benefits of the basic and intermediate package, doubled. Choose any day, any time and the player of our rooster you prefer: you will receive an affirmative answer within a few hours.
