Chester Bennington

Phoenix, 20 marzo 1976 – Palos Verdes Estates, 20 luglio 2017

About

  • 1976 - Born in Phoenix, Arizona
  • 1993 - Began singing with a band called Sean Dowdell and His Friends?
  • 1996 - Married his first wife, Samantha Marie Olt
  • 1999 - Had a successful audition with Linkin Park, who were then called Xero
  • 2000 - Linkin Park released their debut album, Hybrid Theory
  • 2002 - Had his first child, Draven Sebastian
  • 2003 - Linkin Park released Meteora
  • 2006 - Marry his second wife, Talinda Ann Bentley. Had his second child, Tyler Lee Bennington
  • 2011 - Had twins Lilly and Lila
  • 2017 - Was found dead by his housekeeper. His death was ruled a suicide by hanging
